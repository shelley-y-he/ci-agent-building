## Summary
Supplementary appendix to the main SDID paper, providing placebo study implementation details (CPS and Penn World Table datasets, assignment model construction) and simulation tables comparing SC and DIFP estimators with and without regularization across a range of designs.

## Relevance
Contains the empirical and simulation details underlying the robustness checks reported in the main Arkhangelsky et al. (2021) SDID paper; useful if replicating or extending their placebo/simulation results.
